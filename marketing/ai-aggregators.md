## Tips for Better Listings

1. **Use Keywords:** Include "AI", "Dutch", "SMB/MKB", "business tools", "automation"

2. **Highlight Free Tools:** "7 free tools" catches attention

3. **Unique Angle:** "Pay per use, no subscription" differentiates from SaaS

4. **Localization:** Emphasize Dutch market focus - it's unique

5. **Social Proof:** Add user count/testimonials when available

6. **Screenshots:** Show the tool IN ACTION, not just the homepage

7. **Response Time:** Check listings daily for questions/reviews

8. **Cross-promote:** Link aggregator listings in your footer
